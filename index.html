My journey into engineering wasn't linear. I started with a focus on pre-med, volunteering as an EMT for the Charlottesville-Albemarle Rescue Squad. That high-pressure environment taught me rapid problem-solving, but I realized my true passion lay in the technology that powers modern systems.

I pivoted to Electrical Engineering, where I now focus on VLSI and hardware architecture. Beyond the lab, I serve as the Audio Engineer for my rock band, blending my technical skills with creative expression to manage live mixes.
